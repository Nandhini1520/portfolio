Bio.

Currently working as Head of the Department for Information Science and Engineering in Bannari Amman Institute of Technology, Sathyamangalam, Erode, Tamil Nadu with a teaching experience of 7 years. I completed B.Tech Information Technology in Sri Krishna College of Engineering and M.E Computer Science and Engineering in PSG College of Technology with a GATE score of 97.34 percentile. Also, I'm pursuing PhD from Anna University in Information and Communication Engineering and presently working on utility mining. I have been awarded with “The Dharshan award” continuously 4 times for academic excellence and ranked 8th in the Anna University. Also, honoured with a Gold medal for Best Outgoing student at PSG College of Technology. She has worked as an intern in Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore and also, have successfully completed a funded project funded by IEI, Kolkata. As an academician, I have published papers in 6 International / 2 National Conferences and 7 papers in international journals with 3 papers being Scopus indexed. Further, I was ranked as one among top 10% programmers on HackerEarth in the year 2017. I have been trained by TCS in the stream of “UNIX/CPP/RDBMS” in the year 2015. In continuation to that, along with TCS, I led a team of 12 faculty members and have trained around 1400+ TCS-placed students to make them deployable in TCS under pre-ILP training programme for three years.
