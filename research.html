Research

My research focuses on the problem of mining high utility item sets over uncertain databases. As one of the most fundamental issues of uncertainty in data, the problem of mining high utility item sets from such databases has attracted much attention in the data mining communities. Surprisingly, not all frequent item sets are profitable and not all profitable item sets are frequent. So, designing a novel algorithm to mine the high profitable (utility) item set out of unstructured data is the major objective of this research.
